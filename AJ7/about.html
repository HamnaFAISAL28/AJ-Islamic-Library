About Us

Welcome to AJ Islamic Library. I'm Ahmad Jawad, a science student from Pakistan, sharing reflections from the Qur'an, authentic Ahadees, and daily life. This channel offers inspirational Qur’an recitations in collaboration with Hafiz Arham Awan and motivational Islamic reminders rooted in sincerity and scholarly consensus. We focus on sharing naseeha (advice) backed by perpetual practice and avoid complex jurisprudential debates. All Hadith references follow the international numbering system from AhleSunnat collections. Our content is in Urdu, with English translations available for selected videos. All videos are in HD (1080p) with nasheed-style vocals only—no music. Follow @AJIslamicLibrary across YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok.
